EPICUS is a fantasy strategy board game played on a collectible leather tabletop with a unique silkscreened grid. Forget computers — this is real-world strategy, alliances, betrayal, and arcane chaos.

Form temporary alliances, then break them in glorious fashion. Coerce your friends to “help you kill the others”… until it’s time to settle the score.

Will the wizard favor you on your next roll? Will he ignore you? Or worse — curse your path?
Only the wizard knows…

Cast powerful spells, gain your own magical abilities, surround enemy troops, fire arrows across the battlefield, and transport forces into place before unleashing your final attack.

Custom-designed, lead-free pewter pieces make up your army, carefully crafted for durability and feel. The hand-silkscreened leather board ensures every match feels as premium as it does unpredictable.

Every round changes. Every outcome surprises.
EPICUS doesn't just test your strategy — it reveals your true nature.
